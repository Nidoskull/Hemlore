# MacIntosh ó Fhearann an Chapaill-Oighir

## Description
**MacIntosh** the **[Timburr](Species.Timburr)** is an allied explorer as part of [Team Orchard](Team.Orchard). First encountered during [Joint Mission 4](Report.Joint4), his name was chosen by [Amby](Char.Amby%20the%20Marshtomp). He uses he/him pronouns and used to be known as Timburr #305.

MacIntosh speaks with a **very** strong [Tyndrian](Region.Tyndria) accent, and almost certainly hails from somewhere in the direction of [Galtunar](Region.Galtunar).

He speaks at minimum [Satojiran](Region.Satojiri), though likely also speaks [Galtunaric](Region.Galtunar) and potentially [Tyndrian](Region.Tyndria).

## Relationships
MacIntosh has a mother and father, and, presumably, at minimum 304 siblings. Which is a lot.

As member of Team Orchard, presumably he's friends with them.
