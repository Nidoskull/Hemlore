# Wigglytuff's Guild
![A white badge with the letters EX stamped on it.](BadgeWigglytuffsGuild.png)

Wigglytuff's Guild is a [Guild](Mech.Guild) located in [Treasure City](City.Treasure%20City). Founded by [Grandmaster Wigglytuff](Char.Grandmaster%20Wigglytuff), it is currently under leadership of [Guildmaster Blaziken](Char.Blaziken%20(Team%20Aquaflame)).
Wigglytuff's Guild focuses mainly on exploration and rescue, but is authorized to take on Peacekeeper bounties.
