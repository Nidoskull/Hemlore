# Gambit the Pawniard
![A Pawniard glares at the camera, looking cool. He wears a blue bandana like a cape.](assets/image/IconGambit.png)

## Description
**Gambit** the **[Pawniard](Pawniard%20(Species))** is a rookie explorer as part of [Team Nova](Team%20Nova). He is 15 years old and hails from [Isshu](Isshu), having moved frequently in his youth.

Gambit is rather [Adamant](Nature) and wears his blue Team bandanna like a cape. He lacks the dark circles under the eyes of the average Pawniard, and has a small, green hexagon on the back of his neck from his time merged with [Zygarde](Zygarde%20(Myth)). 

Gambit speaks [Satojiran](Satojiri), though with an [Isshun](Isshu) accent, and of course fluently speaks modern [Isshun](Isshu).

## Relationships
Known relatives are:
- [Kasparov the Bisharp](Kasparov%20the%20Bisharp) (Father)
- [Judit](Judit%20the%20Kingambit) (Mother)
- [Hikaru](Hikaru%20the%20Pawniard) (Sister)

As founding member of Team Nova, Gambit is of course close with [Hemlock the Venipede](Hemlock%20the%20Venipede), [Neon the Tynamo](Neon%20the%20Tynamo), [Aemilia the Rookidee](Aemilia%20the%20Rookidee) and [Sheo the Gastly](Sheo%20the%20Gastly). Additionally, Gambit has been named as [Legate Honorus](Honorum) by [Zygarde](Zygarde%20(Myth)). 

## History
Gambit was born to a squadron, laid by [Kingambit](Kingambit%20(Gambit%27s%20Mother)) and sired by [Kasparov](Kasparov%20the%20Bisharp). The squadron he belonged to did not usually remain in one place for long, preferring to remain mobile and mobilized.
