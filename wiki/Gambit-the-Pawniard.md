# Gambit the Pawniard
![A Pawniard glares at the camera, looking cool. He wears a blue bandana like a cape.](assets/image/IconGambit.png)

## Description
**Gambit** the **[Pawniard](Pawniard-(Species))** is a rookie explorer as part of [Team Nova](Team-Nova). He is 15 years old and hails from [Isshu](Isshu), having moved frequently in his youth.

Gambit is rather [Adamant](Nature) and wears his blue Team bandanna like a cape. He lacks the dark circles under the eyes of the average Pawniard, and has a small, green hexagon on the back of his neck from his time merged with [Zygarde](Zygarde-(Myth)). 

Gambit speaks [Satojiran](Satojiri), though with an [Isshun](Isshu) accent, and of course fluently speaks modern [Isshun](Isshu).

## Relationships
Known relatives are:
- [Kasparov the Bisharp](Kasparov-the-Bisharp) (Father)
- [Kingambit](Kingambit-(Gambit%27s-Mother)) (Mother)

As founding member of Team Nova, Gambit is of course close with [Hemlock the Venipede](Hemlock-the-Venipede), [Neon the Tynamo](Neon-the-Tynamo), [Aemilia the Rookidee](Aemilia-the-Rookidee) and [Sheo the Gastly](Sheo-the-Gastly). Additionally, Gambit has been named as [Legate Honorus](Honorum) by [Zygarde](Zygarde-(Myth)). 

## History
Gambit was born to a squadron, laid by [Kingambit](Kingambit-(Gambit%27s-Mother)) and sired by [Kasparov](Kasparov-the-Bisharp). The squadron he belonged to did not usually remain in one place for long, preferring to remain mobile and mobilized.
