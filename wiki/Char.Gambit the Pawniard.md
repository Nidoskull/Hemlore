# Gambit, Son of Judit, Son of Kasparov
![A Pawniard glares at the camera, looking cool. He wears a blue bandana like a cape.](assets/image/IconGambit.png)

## Description
**Gambit** the **[Pawniard](Species.Pawniard)** is a rookie explorer as part of [Team Nova](Team.Nova). He is 15 years old and hails from [Isshu](Region.Isshu), having moved frequently in his youth.

Gambit is rather [Adamant](Mech.Nature) and wears his blue Team bandanna like a cape. He lacks the dark circles under the eyes of the average Pawniard, and has a small, green hexagon on the back of his neck from his time merged with [Zygarde](Tale.Zygarde). 

Gambit speaks [Satojiran](Region.Satojiri), though with an [Isshun](Region.Isshu) accent, and of course fluently speaks modern [Isshun](Region.Isshu).

## History
Gambit was born to a squadron, laid by [Judit](Char.Judit%20the%20Kingambit) and sired by [Kasparov](Char.Kasparov%20the%20Bisharp). The squadron he belonged to did not usually remain in one place for long, preferring to remain mobile and mobilized.
