# Georio, the Geo Brother
## Description
**Georio** the **Alo'lolan [Geodude](Species.Geodude)** is an ally explorer as part of [Team Orchard](Team.Orchard). First seen during [Orchard mission... 3?](Report.Orchard3) (citation needed), he hails from [Saltdepth Caverns](MD.Saltdepth%20Caverns). He uses he/him pronouns.

Georio is Very Red. Even the iron shavings that give him eyebrows and 'hair' are slightly red-tinged. It is unknown why he is Red.

Georio speaks [Satojiran](Region.Satojiri).

## Relationships
Known relatives are:
- [Geoigi](Char.Geoigi%the%20Geodude) (Brother) (do not separate)
- Geo Bros's Mother (Mother)
- Geo Bros's Father (Father)

As part of Team Orchard, Geoigi is friends with [Amby](Char.Amby%20the%20Marshtomp), [Mint](Char.Mint%the%20Chespin), [Pom](Char.Pom%20the%20Honedge), [Gala](Char.Gala%20the%20Morelull), [Elma](Char.Elma%20(Matcha)), [Macintosh](Char.Macintosh%20the%20Timburr), and [Jazz](Char.Jazz%20the%20Runerigus).
