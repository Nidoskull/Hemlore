# Gambit the Pawniard
![A Pawniard glares at the camera, looking cool. He wears a blue bandana like a cape.](assets/image/IconGambit.png)

## Description
**Gambit** the **[Pawniard](Pawniard_(Species))** is a rookie explorer as part of [Team_Nova](Team_Nova). He is 15 years old and hails from [Isshu](Isshu), having moved frequently in his youth.

Gambit is rather [Adamant](Nature) and wears his blue Team bandanna like a cape. He lacks the dark circles under the eyes of the average Pawniard, and has a small, green hexagon on the back of his neck from his time merged with [Zygarde](Zygarde_(Myth)). 

Gambit speaks [Satojiran](Satojiri), though with an [Isshun](Isshu) accent, and of course fluently speaks modern [Isshun](Isshu).

## Relationships
Known relatives are:
- [Kasparov the Bisharp](Kasparov_the_Bisharp) (Father)
- [Kingambit](Kingambit_(Gambit%27s_Mother)) (Mother)

As founding member of Team Nova, Gambit is of course close with [Hemlock_the_Venipede](Hemlock_the_Venipede), [Neon_the_Tynamo](Neon_the_Tynamo), [Aemilia_the_Rookidee](Aemilia_the_Rookidee) and [Sheo_the_Gastly](Sheo_the_Gastly). Additionally, Gambit has been named as [Legate Honorus](Honorum) by [Zygarde](Zygarde_(Myth)). 

## History
Gambit was born to a squadron, laid by [Kingambit](Kingambit_(Gambit%27s_Mother)) and sired by [Kasparov](Kasparov_the_Bisharp). The squadron he belonged to did not usually remain in one place for long, preferring to remain mobile and mobilized.
