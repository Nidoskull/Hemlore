# Orbs
**Wonder Orbs**, usually just called Orbs, are a category of item. Each variety of **orb** acts differently. Some target yourself, others target your whole Team (regardless of distance), some target an enemy, others target the whole floor of enemies, and yet others target the floor a bit more literally.

Some Orbs have similar effects to certain [Wands](Item.Wands), such as the Blowback Orb and the Whirlwind Wand.

### Miscellaneous Orbs
- **Cleanse Orb** - When used, cleans your Grimy items. Has no effect on Grimy Food... - This Orb is made a soft, flaky pink stone. Eating it is toxic.
- **Drought Orb** - When used, it drains liquid from the floor. It has no effect on underwater Dungeons. - This Orb is made of transparent glass with a small metal drain on its top. Using it *sluuurps* all the liquid into it, and then it disappears!
- **Hail Orb** - When used, changes the [Weather](Mech.Weather) to Hail on the floor. - This Orb is made of light blue frosted glass. It feels really, really cold to the touch.
- **One-Room Orb** - When used, it will tear down all walls on the floor, turning the floor into one giant room. This *will* trigger any Monster Houses on the floor.
- **Sunny Orb** - When used, changes the [Weather](Mech.Weather) to Sunny on the floor. - This Orb is made of orange glass and glows faintly in the dark. It's warm and dry to the touch.
