# Pom the Honedge
## Description
**Pom** the **[Honedge](Species.Honedge)** is an adventurer that's part of [Team Orchard](Team.Orchard) as part of the [Glaive Expedition Federation](Guild.Glaive). He uses he/him pronouns.

Pom has an [Eerie Pendant](Item.Pendants) wrapped around its pommel, and additionally has a small teal bag tied to its grip.

He speaks [Satojiran](Region.Satojiri).

## Relationships
As a founding member of Team Orchard, Pom is friends with [Amby](Char.Amby%20the%20Marshtomp), [Mint](Char.Mint%20the%20Chespin), [Gala](Char.Gala%20the%20Morelull), [Elma](Char.Elma%20the%20Poltchageist), [Georio](Char.Georio%20the%20Geodude), [Geogi](Char.Geoigi%20the%20Geodude), [Ishy](Char.Ishy%20the%20Fuecoco), [Jazz](Char.Jazz%20the%20Runerigus), and [Macintosh](Char.Macintosh%20the%20Timburr).
