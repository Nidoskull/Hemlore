# Isshuana Dirge III
![A Fuecoco greets the camera. It wears a brown fedora.](assets/image/IconAllyIshy.png)
## Description
**Ishy** the **[Fuecoco](Fuecoco%20(Species))** is assistant assistant Librarian in the [Glaive Exploration Federation](Glaive%20Exploration%20Federation), and an ally explorer as part of [Team Orchard](Team%20Orchard). First encountered on Team Orchard's mission to [Mount Maw](Mount%20Maw), Ishy is 14 years old.

Ishy is almost always seen wearing a brown fedora, like his father and his grandfather before him.

Ishy is fluent in seven languages, one of which being [Satojiran](Satojiri).

## Relationships
Known relatives are:
- Isshuana Dirge I (Grandfather)
- Isshuana Dirge II (Father)
- Ishy's Mother (Mother)

As member of Team Orchard, Ishy is of course friends with [Mint](Mint%the%20Chespin), [Amby](Amby%20the%20Mudkip), [Pom](Pom%20the%20Honedge), [Gala](Gala%20the%20Morelull), [Elma](Elma%20the%20Poltchageist), [Georio](Georio%20the%20Geodude), and [Geogi](Geogi%20the%20Geodude).

[Hemlock](Hemlock%20the%20Venipede) and Ishy have bonded over being nerds.
