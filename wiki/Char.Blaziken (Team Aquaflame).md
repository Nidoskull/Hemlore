## Description
**[Blaziken](Species.Torchic)** holds the title of Guildmaster in [Wigglytuff's Guild](Wigglytuff%27s%20Guild). He has, presumably, also been known as Combusken and Torchic. He uses he/him pronouns.

Blaziken has been titled Exploratores Temporis-Tenebrarum by [Princeps Maris Caeli-Explorator](Princeps%20Maris%20Caeli-Explorator). He's thirty years old.

Blaziken speaks fluent [Satojiran](Region.Satojiri), and may speak other languages.

## Relationships
Blaziken is part of [Team Aquaflame](Team.Aquaflame) alongside [Feraligatr](Char.Feraligatr%20(Team%20Aquaflame)).

He appears to be chill with [Darkrai](Tale.Darkrai).
