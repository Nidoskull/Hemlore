# Tome of Tongues
The Tome of Tongues is a relic created originally by [Uxie](Tale.Uxie) and [Kirlia of the Twelve](Char.Original%20Kirlia). According to Zygarde, it was used to create language.

If one carries the Tome of Tongues with them, they can instantaneously comprehend any language, written or spoken, if they can navigate the Tome.
