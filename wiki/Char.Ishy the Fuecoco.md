# Isshuana Dirge III
![A Fuecoco greets the camera. It wears a brown fedora.](assets/image/IconAllyIshy.png)
## Description
**Ishy** the **[Fuecoco](Species.Fuecoco)** is assistant assistant Librarian in the [Glaive Expedition Federation](Guild.Glaive%20Expedition%20Federation), and an ally [Explorer](Explorer) as part of [Team Orchard](Team.Orchard). First encountered on Team Orchard's mission to [Mount Maw](MD.Mount%20Maw), Ishy is 14 years old.

Ishy is almost always seen wearing a brown fedora, like his father and his grandfather before him.

Ishy is fluent in seven languages, one of which being [Satojiran](Region.Satojiri).

## Relationships
Known relatives are:
- Isshuana Dirge I (Grandfather)
- Isshuana Dirge II (Father)
- Ishy's Mother (Mother)

As member of Team Orchard, Ishy is of course friends with [Mint](Char.Mint%20the%20Chespin), [Amby](Char.Amby%20the%20Mudkip), [Pom](Char.Pom%20the%20Honedge), [Gala](Char.Gala%20the%20Morelull), [Elma](Char.Elma%20the%20Poltchageist), [Georio](Char.Georio%20the%20Geodude), and [Geogi](Char.Geogi%20the%20Geodude).

[Hemlock](Char.Hemlock%20of%20Belladonna) and Ishy have bonded over being nerds.
