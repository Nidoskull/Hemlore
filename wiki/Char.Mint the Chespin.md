## Description
**Mint** the **[Chespin](Species.Chespin)** is an explorer as part of [Team Orchard](Team.Orchard). He is nearly thirty years old, uses he/him pronouns and is a friend of Zygarde. His full name is, at minimum, Mint, father of Brook, father of Penny, father of Castan, son of Candice and Sorrel.

Unlike the average Chespin, Mint is *incredibly* small. His eyes are blue, he has several patches of orange on his grassy coat, and his quills are almost always flexed. He almost always wears his Team Bag.

He speaks [Satojiran](Region.Satojiri), [Alo'lolan](Region.Alo%27lola) and [Galtunaric](Region.Galtunar).
