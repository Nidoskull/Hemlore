# Isshuana Dirge III
![A Fuecoco greets the camera. It wears a brown fedora.](assets/image/IconAllyIshy.png)
## Description
**Ishy** the **[Fuecoco](Fuecoco-(Species))** is assistant assistant Librarian in the [Glaive Exploration Federation](Glaive-Exploration-Federation), and an ally explorer as part of [Team-Orchard](Team-Orchard). First encountered on Team Orchard's mission to [Mount-Maw](Mount-Maw), Ishy is 14 years old.

Ishy is almost always seen wearing a brown fedora, like his father and his grandfather before him.

Ishy is fluent in seven languages, one of which being [Satojiran](Satojiri).

## Relationships
Known relatives are:
- Isshuana Dirge I (Grandfather)
- Isshuana Dirge II (Father)
- Ishy's Mother (Mother)

As member of Team Orchard, Ishy is of course friends with [Mint](Mint-the-Chespin), [Amby](Amby-the-Mudkip), [Pom](Pom-the-Honedge), [Gala](Gala-the-Morelull), [Elma](Elma-the-Poltchageist), [Georio](Georio-the-Geodude), and [Geogi](Geogi-the-Geodude).

[Hemlock](Hemlock-the-Venipede) and Ishy have bonded over being nerds.
