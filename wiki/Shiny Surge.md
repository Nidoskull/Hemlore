# Shiny Surge

Shiny Surge is a special ability specific to [Shiny](Shiny) Pokemon, granted by [Victini](Victini%20(Myth)), allowing them to do a special action during a battle in dungeon.

## Known Effects
A Shiny Surge's effect can vary from getting a super-effective move in a pinch, to surviving a KO, to granting an XP boost for the entire team; and can be used in tandem with another's shiny surge for a much greater effect. If used to avoid fainting, the user only gets half the xp after battle.

- Immunity to all Status moves for one turn
- Temporary Ability change
- Temporary learning of a move from later in the evo line
- Temporary learning of a Legendary Signature Move
- Revival to full health
- Guarantee a critical hit
- Return a life unfairly stolen

## Notable uses

- [Neon the Tynamo](Neon%20the%20Tynamo) and [Ladon the Dipplin](Ladon%20the%20Dipplin) combined their power to bring Basilinna Matidia to life during [Nova Mission #3](Report.Nova%20Mission%203)
