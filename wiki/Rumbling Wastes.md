# Rumbling Wastes (Dungeon)
The **Rumbling Wastes** is a [Mystery Dungeon](Mystery%20Dungeon) in [Satojiri](Satojiri), consisting of 10 floors aboveground.
It is a sweeping plateau of sun-bleached and lightning-struck earth.

This Dungeon mainly contains Electric-Type Pokémon, but additionally has Fire, Water, Dragon, Fighting, Normal, and Ground types.
At the peak of the Wastes stands Iron Hands, Guardian of the [Lightning Oni](Thundurus%20(Myth)), guarding the Electric [Protean Ingredient](Protean%20Ingredient), Pure Storm Essence.
