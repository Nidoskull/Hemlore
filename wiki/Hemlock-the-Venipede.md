# Hemlock, child of Belladonna
![A polite Venipede. It has a blue Team Ribbon tied around its left antenna, and its shoulder markings are similar to parentheses. It is looking at you with wide-open eyes.](assets/image/IconHemlock.png)
## General biography
**Hemlock** the **[Venipede](Venipede-(Species))** is Assistant [Librarian](Librarian) to [Relicanth](Relicanth-(Glaive-Expedition-Federation)) in the [Glaive-Expedition-Federation](Glaive-Expedition-Federation), alongside a [Rookie](Rookie) [Explorer](Explorer) as part of [Team-Nova](Team-Nova). It is 14 years old and hails from [Paleo City](Paleo-City). 

Hemlock is rather [Timid](Nature) , and often lost in thought. It is unlabeled on the gender, romance and sexuality axes.
During its adventures, it has gained a [Tera Factor](Terastalization) of +1.
It follows the teachings of [Uxie, Mindborne of Knowledge](Uxie-(Mythology)). 

It speaks [Satojiran](Satojiri), alongside comprehending [Alo'lolan](Alo'lola). It possesses a [Tome of Tongues](Tome-of-Tongues).

## Relationships
Known relatives are:
- [Volcarona](Volcarona-(Hemlock%27s-Grandfather)) (Grandfather)
- [Belladonna the Scolipede](Belladonna-the-Scolipede) (Mother)
- [Foxglove the Venipede](Foxglove-the-Venipede) (Sibling)
- [Nightshade the Venonat](Nightshade-the-Venonat) (Sibling)
- [Hemlock's Father](Hemlock's-Father) (Father)

As one of Team Nova's founding members, it is good friends with [Neon-the-Tynamo](Neon-the-Tynamo), [Gambit-the-Pawniard](Gambit-the-Pawniard), [Aemilia-the-Rookidee](Aemilia-the-Rookidee) and [Sheo-the-Gastly](Sheo-the-Gastly).

Back in Archa City, it was part of a book club with [Dusty the Cubone](Dusty-the-Cubone) and [Buizel](Buizel-(Paleo-City)).

Because of various reasons, Hemlock doesn't really like the [Pan Trio](Pan-Trio) or [Cofagrigus](Cofagrigus-(Glorious-Gold)).

## History
Hemlock lived in Paleo City for most of its childhood. Its mother, Belladonna, sent it to the Glaive Expedition Federation to aid it in its quest for knowledge, and to grow stronger than it could within the safe walls of its home.
