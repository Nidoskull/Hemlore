# Hemlock, child of Belladonna
![[shime1.png]]
## General biography
**Hemlock** the **[Venipede](/wiki/Venipede%20(Species))** is Assistant [Librarian](/wiki/Librarian) to [Relicanth](/wiki/Relicanth%20(Glaive%20Expedition%20Federation) in the [Glaive Expedition Federation](/wiki/Glaive%20Expedition%20Federation), alongside a [Rookie](/wiki/Rookie) [Explorer](/wiki/Explorer) as part of [Team Nova](/wiki/Team%20Nova). It is 14 years old and hails from [Paleo City](/wiki/Paleo%20City). 

Hemlock is rather [Timid](/wiki/Nature) , and often lost in thought.
During its adventures, it has gained a [Tera Factor](/wiki/Terastalization) of +1.
It follows the teachings of [Uxie, Mindborne of Knowledge](/wiki/Uxie). 

It speaks [Satojiran](/wiki/Satojiri), alongside comprehending [Alo'lolan](/wiki/Alo'lola). It possesses a [Tome of Tongues](/wiki/Tome%20of%20Tongues).

Known relatives are:
- [Belladonna the Scolipede](/wiki/Belladonna%20the%20Scolipede) (Mother)
- [Foxglove the Venipede](/wiki/Foxglove%20the%20Venipede) (Sibling)
- [Nightshade the Venonat](/wiki/Nightshade%20the%20Venonat) (Sibling)
- [Hemlock's Father](/wiki/Hemlock's%20Father) (Father)

As one of Team Nova's founding members, it is good friends with [Neon the Tynamo](/wiki/Neon%20the%20Tynamo), [Gambit the Pawniard](/wiki/Gambit%20the%20Pawniard), [Aemilia the Rookidee](/wiki/Aemilia%20the%20Rookidee) and [Sheo the Gastly](/wiki/Sheo%20the%20Gastly).
