# Secluded Coast
Secluded Coast is a [Mystery Dungeon](Mech.Mystery%20Dungeon) near [Archa City](City.Archa%20City) in [Satojiri](Region.Satojiri), consisting of 9 floors belowground.

As its name implies, it is a secluded coastline.

It is recommended to be level 12 or higher when taking on this dungeon.
