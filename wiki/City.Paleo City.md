# Paleo City
**Paleo City** is a city in **[Satojiri](Region.Satojiri)** built inside of the crater formed by [Victini's](Tale.Victini) crash-landing.

The city's population is comprised mainly of Fossil Pokémon and Bug-type Pokémon. 

## Glaive Expedition Federation involvement
Hemlock is originally from this city.
