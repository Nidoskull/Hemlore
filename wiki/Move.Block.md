**Block** is a non-damaging [Normal-type](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Normal_\(type\) "Normal (type)") [move](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Move "Move") first discovered in [Hasorem](Region.Hasorem).

It has differing effects when used on an ally than when used on a foe. 
- When used on a foe, you **block** them from dodging the next incoming attack until the start of your next turn. This makes the next move to target them guaranteed to hit.
- When used on an ally, you **block** an opposing move from hitting them, similar to the [IQ Talent](Mech.IQ) Bodyguard.
