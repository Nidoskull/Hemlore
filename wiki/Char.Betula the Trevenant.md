## Description
**Betula** the **[Trevenant](Species.Phantump)** is [Mestor Wormwood](Char.Mestor%20Wormwood)'s wife. First seen during [Nova's time adventure](Report.Nova3), she is at least six thousand years old and hails from [Carew City](Carew%20City). She uses she/her pronouns.

Betula's outer shell is comprised of birch wood, and her leaves are orange.

She speaks Satojiran at minimum.
