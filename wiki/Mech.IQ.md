# Illumina Quality (IQ)
Illumina Quality, usually shortened to **IQ**, is a measure of one's strengths and unique traits. The higher your IQ, the likelier it is that you're capable of some unique traits. 

[Gummis](Item.Gummi) are the easiest method to raise your IQ.

## List of IQ Talents
_This list is incomplete. You can help by expanding it._
- **Bodyguard** - If an ally is at 25% or less HP, you can choose to jump in front of an attack meant for them.
- **Charismatic** - Effect unknown (to me).
- **Hyper-Mobile** - Cannot be paralyzed or have your Speed lowered by any means. You will always avoid a teammate's attacks, even if they do not have Nontraitor.
- **Item Seeker** - Detect Secret Treasure hidden in dungeons. If you have multiple Pokémon with this Talent on a team, this stacks.
- **Nontraitor** - You will never attack your allies, even when confused. You will resist usage of a Command Wand on you.
- **Orb Specialist** - You can use one [Orb](Item.Orb) per [Floor](Mech.Mystery%20Dungeon) as a free action.
- **Pokémon Seeker** - You will know if a floor has a [Monster House](Mech.Mystery%20Dungeon) or a [Tera Pokémon](Terastalization), though you will not know where they might be.
- **Self-Curer** - You have a chance to heal your own status effects at the end of your turn. You will always heal from them at the end of a battle.
- **Sleepless** - You will not fall asleep, even by moves such as Rest.
- **Survivalist** - When eating [Grimy Food](Item.Grimy%20Food) or drinking [Odd Serum](Item.Odd%20Serum), you can choose between two options.
- **Thrown Specialist** - Deal more damage when throwing or hurling items.
- **Tile Seeker** - You can detect hidden [Effect Tiles](Mech.Effect%20Tiles) in the floor of a Dungeon, but you cannot know the effects.
- **Wand Specialist** - Wands are less likely to break, and you can use one as a free action at the start of any battle.
