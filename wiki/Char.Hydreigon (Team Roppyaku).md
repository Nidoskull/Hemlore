# Hydreigon, father of Zweilous and Champion of Dialga
## Description
**[Hydreigon](Species.Deino)** is a professional Treasure Appraiser in [Archa City](City.Archa%20City). He has also been known as Zweilous and Deino.

Hydreigon's scales shimmer with a color not unlike [the One Who Breathes Time](Tale.Dialga). 

He speaks at minimum [Satojiran](Region.Satojiri), though likely has more-than-passing knowledge of various other languages.

## Relationships
Known relatives are:
- [Hydreigon](Char.Hydreigon%20(Team%20Profundo)) (Wife? Ex-wife?)
- [Zweilous](Char.Zweilous%20(Archa%20City)) (Daughter)

He is friends with [Guildmaster Baxcalibur](Char.Guildmaster%20Baxcalibur) and [Volcarona](Char.Volcarona%20(Team%20Roppyaku)). He is, assumedly, also friends with [Rillaboom](Char.Rillaboom%20(Team%20Profundo)).
