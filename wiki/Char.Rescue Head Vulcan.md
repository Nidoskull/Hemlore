## Description
**Vulcan** the **[Charizard](Species.Charmander)** is Head of a Rescue Organization in the [Eastern Peninsula](Region.Eastern%20Peninsula). He was first seen in person when he visited the Guild. He uses he/him pronouns.

Vulcan wears a blue Rescue Scarf with a Rescue Badge pinned to it at the base of his neck.

He speaks [Satojiran](Region.Satojiri).

## Relationships
Vulcan is, presumably, at *minimum* good friends with [Rescue Head Sakura](Char.Rescue%20Head%20Sakura).
