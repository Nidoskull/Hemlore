# Isshuana Dirge III
![A Fuecoco greets the camera. It wears a brown fedora.](assets/image/IconAllyIshy.png)
## Description
**Ishy** the **[Fuecoco](Fuecoco_(Species))** is assistant assistant Librarian in the [Glaive Exploration Federation](Glaive_Exploration_Federation), and an ally explorer as part of [Team_Orchard](Team_Orchard). First encountered on Team Orchard's mission to [Mount_Maw](Mount_Maw), Ishy is 14 years old.

Ishy is almost always seen wearing a brown fedora, like his father and his grandfather before him.

Ishy is fluent in seven languages, one of which being [Satojiran](Satojiri).

## Relationships
Known relatives are:
- Isshuana Dirge I (Grandfather)
- Isshuana Dirge II (Father)
- Ishy's Mother (Mother)

As member of Team Orchard, Ishy is of course friends with [Mint](Mint_the_Chespin), [Amby](Amby_the_Mudkip), [Pom](Pom_the_Honedge), [Gala](Gala_the_Morelull), [Elma](Elma_the_Poltchageist), [Georio](Georio_the_Geodude), and [Geogi](Geogi_the_Geodude).

[Hemlock](Hemlock_the_Venipede) and Ishy have bonded over being nerds.
