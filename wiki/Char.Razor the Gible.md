## Description
**Razor** the **[Gible](Species.Gible)** is an ally explorer as part of Team Stamp Squad. Recruited on [Stamp Squad Mission 2](Report.StampSquad2), it hails from [Mount Maw](MD.Mount%20Maw). She is also known as Chompy Friend (to itself) or Big Friend (to Hemlock). It has no idea how old she is.

Razor is an Alpha, and her eyes glimmer red to prove it. She has a [Gar-Chunk](Exclusive%20items) that it cradles while she sleeps.

Despite its apparent youth, Razor speaks [Satojiran](Region.Satojiri).

## Relationships
Razor has no known relatives.

Razor is a part of Team Stamp Squad, and would chomp for [Thinky Friend](Kasi%20the%20Ralts), [Hammer Friend](Char.Titania%20the%20Tinkatink), and [Cold Friend](Char.Ralmin%20the%20Bergmite). She also likes [Rawr Friend](Mars%20the%20Larvitar), [Stabby Friend](Char.Gambit%20the%20Pawniard), [Spiky Friend](Mint%20the%20Chespin), [Fluffy Friend](Volt%20the%20Shinx), [Smart Friend](Char.Hemlock%20of%20Belladonna)... the list goes on.

## History
Very little is known about Razor's past. It was recruited by Team Stamp Squad in Mount Maw by being fed a Pecha Berry, and has since gone on many a mission.
